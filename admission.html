General Application Procedures

All applications into Non-Degree, Undergraduates and Postgraduates are performed online via the Admission System www.zumis.ac.tz/admission.

The academic year begins in October and ends in September of the following year. During registration, documents are thoroughly checked. Prospective students must bring official original transcripts, certificates, and other relevant documents as declared on the application forms. Legal action shall be taken against any applicant who presents forged documents.

No change of names is allowed during registration or the course of study; students may only use names appearing on their Ordinary Level Certificate.

Application Fees

  • Non-Degree: TZS 25,000 (Tanzanians), USD 25 (Foreigners).
  • Undergraduate: TZS 35,000 (Tanzanians), USD 35 (Foreigners).
  • Postgraduate: TZS 50,000 (Tanzanians), USD 50 (Foreigners).

Selection Procedure

  1. Selection follows TCU and NACTE guidelines.
  2. Academic units may impose additional admission criteria.
  3. Authorized Board reviews qualifications and approves selected candidates.
  4. Admissions Office notifies selected applicants with relevant details.

Registration of Students

  1. Registration occurs within 14 days from semester start.
  2. Late registration after two weeks is not permitted.
  3. Proof of financial capability is required.
  4. Registration fees are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  5. University Entrance Examination may be required.
  6. Medical report submission is mandatory.
  7. Valid NHIF identity is required.
  8. Foreign students must submit a Police Clearance Certificate.
  9. Tanzanian students submit 2 passport photos; Foreign students submit 6.
  10. Continuing students must register in the first two weeks or pay TZS 50,000 fine.
  11. Legal action will be taken for fraudulent admissions.

Registration for Previously Discontinued Students

  1. Students discontinued on academic grounds may apply in a different field after evaluation.
  2. Students discontinued on disciplinary grounds may reapply after two years.
  3. Discontinued students cannot transfer credits but may register for a different program in the next cycle.
