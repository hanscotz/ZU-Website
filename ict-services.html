Welcome to the official webpage of the Information Technology (IT) Unit at Zanzibar University. We are delighted to have you here. The IT Unit plays a vital role in supporting the university’s mission through the provision of reliable, secure, and innovative technology services. Whether you are a student, staff member, researcher, or guest, our team is committed to delivering high-quality IT support and solutions that enhance teaching, learning, research, and administration. Explore our services and access useful resources. We are here to empower your digital journey at Zanzibar University. Your success is our priority — welcome aboard!

Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a model IT Services Unit that consistently delivers innovative, reliable, and secure technology solutions, thereby enabling Zanzibar University to excel in its mission of acquiring and disseminating knowledge.

Through our commitment to excellence, we strive to serve as a catalyst for academic and administrative success within the university community and make a positive impact on society as a whole.

There is a wealth of material to support learning and research at the University; over 10,000 printed volumes and an extensive range of suitable electronic books. There is a Child Protection Resource Center sponsored by Save the Children Zanzibar Office, which provides access to child protection and child rights governance information. The main library has one floor and a sitting capacity of 200 users at any given time. The library uses Dewey Desimal Classification System (DDC) to arrange resources on shelves

🛠️ Our Core IT Services

📧 Email Services

Our E-mail service provides a reliable and secure platform for communication within the university community. We offer personalized email accounts to all faculty, and staff, enabling seamless communication and collaboration. Our service includes robust spam filtering, ample storage space, and integration with productivity tools such as calendars. We strive to ensure that our email service is accessible across devices, allowing users to stay connected and organized.

🌐 Network Administration

Our Network Administration service focuses on maintaining a high-performance and secure network infrastructure throughout the university campus. We monitor and manage network resources, including switches, routers, and firewalls, to ensure optimal connectivity and availability. Our team works diligently to troubleshoot network issues, implement necessary upgrades, and enforce security measures to protect sensitive data and prevent unauthorized access. We also provide support for network connectivity and assist with the configuration of network-enabled devices.

💻 Computer Lab Services

Our Computer Laboratories Services aim to provide students and faculty with state-of-the-art computing resources to support their academic and research activities. We maintain and manage computer labs equipped with the latest hardware and software, ensuring a conducive environment for learning and experimentation. Our services include regular maintenance and updates of software applications, user support, and assistance with specialized software and tools.

📶 Connect to Wi-Fi

Our Wi-Fi service ensures reliable and seamless internet connectivity across the university campus. We deploy and maintain a robust wireless network infrastructure that covers all key areas, including classrooms, libraries, and common areas. Students, faculty, and staff can easily connect to the Wi-Fi network using their university credentials, allowing them to access online resources, collaborate, and conduct research.
