Welcome Message from the Institute Director

Dear Students, Faculty, and Visitors,

I am delighted to extend a warm, enthusiastic and heartfelt welcome to the Institute of Continuing Education (ICE) at Zanzibar University! With immense pleasure, I extend my greetings to all of you as we embark on an exciting journey of learning, growth, and personal development.

At the Institute of Continuing Education, we understand that education is a lifelong pursuit, and we are committed to providing you with an enriching and dynamic environment where you can expand your horizons and achieve your academic and professional goals.

Whether you are returning to further your education or taking your first steps toward higher learning, our dedicated faculty and staff are here to support you every step of the way. As a part of Zanzibar University, you are now a member of a vibrant academic community that values diversity, creativity, and innovation. Our programs are designed to equip you with valuable knowledge and skills and inspire you to think critically, engage in meaningful discussions, and contribute positively to society.

Throughout your time at the Institute of Continuing Studies, you will have the opportunity to collaborate with fellow students from various backgrounds, exchange ideas, and build lasting friendships.

Our campus is where ideas flourish and dreams are nurtured, and I encourage you to make the most of the resources and experiences that await you.Please remember that learning is a continuous journey and every challenge you overcome and every achievement you attain will shape your path forward. Be curious, be persistent, and most importantly, believe in yourself and your potential.

Best regards,
Mr. Saleh Said Mwinyi,
Director - Institute of Continuing Education,
Zanzibar University.

Institute Background

The establishment of the Zanzibar University Institute of Continuing Education (ZUICE) is a noteworthy achievement. It came into existence during the 20th session of the University Council on November 30, 2006, and its primary aim is to promote knowledge across various fields through a range of offerings, including short courses, workshops, and training programs.The ICE motto is “striving for a competitive edge in developing and enhancing the skills of human capital for outstanding performance and self-reliance.”

Mission

“At Zanzibar University Institute of Continuing Education, we are dedicated to providing high-quality, ethical education that fosters lifelong learning and professional growth. Our goal is to enhance knowledge, skills, and ethical values, contributing to the betterment of individuals in Tanzania and beyond."

Vision

"To be a renowned center of ethical excellence and lifelong learning, inspiring positive societal changes through innovative education and unwavering commitment to fostering knowledge, skills, and values."
